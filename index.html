  Hello world! My name is Zuri and I am a Bolivian Mexican American Artist! My passion bloomed in middle school when I began my first ceramics course and experimented with digital art (on animal jam.) Now I work in multiple mediums; code, 3D modeling, digital collages, video, dance, and ceramics. I particulalry resonate with the inhuman, especially dogs which is the most common symbol in my art. This is in large part due to my position at the crossection of multiple minorities which I often explore with my art. Bitchgirl is my current passion project which I continue to work on still.
